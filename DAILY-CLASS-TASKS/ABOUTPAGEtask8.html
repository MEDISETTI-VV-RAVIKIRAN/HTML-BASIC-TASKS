HEALTHY EATING Organic Foods What is Organic Food and What are the Benefits? Is organic food really healthier? Is it worth buying organic? Find out what the benefits of organic food are, what the labels mean, and which foods are the best value.

What is organic food? Organic food is produced in compliance with organic farming standards that regulate the way agricultural products are grown and processed. While the regulations vary from country to country, in the U.S., organic crops must be grown without the use of synthetic herbicides, pesticides, and fertilizers, or bioengineered genes (GMOs). Organic livestock raised for meat, eggs, and dairy products must be raised in living conditions accommodating their natural behaviors (such as the ability to graze on pasture) and fed organic feed and forage. They may not be given antibiotics, growth hormones, or any animal by-products.
